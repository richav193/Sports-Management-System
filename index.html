Cricket

Cricket is the most popular sport in India by far and is played everywhere. The Indian national cricket won the 1983 Cricket World Cup, the 2007 ICC World T20, the 2011 Cricket World Cup, the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy, shared 2002 ICC champions trophy with Srilanka. The good news for Indian Cricket fans is the 2023 Cricket World Cup will be hosted by India.

BasketBall

First BasketBall National level tournaments were organized in India in 1934. The India National BasketBall team became a member of FIBA in 1936. The governing body, BFI, was formed in 1950. Poonam Mahajan is the president of BasketBall Federation of India. The current head of BFI is R.S.Gill.

VolleyBall

VolleyBall is a sport played all over in India, both in rural as well as urban India. It is a popular recreation sport. India was ranked 5th in Asia and 27th in the world in 2013. Doing well in the youth and junior levels, India came in second second in the 2003 World Youth Championships. The Indian senior men's team is currently ranked 131st in the world.

FootBall

The India national football team represents India in international football and is controlled by the All India Football Federation. Under the global jurisdiction of FIFA and governed in Asia by the AFC, the team is also part of the South Asian Football Federation. The team, which was once considered one of the best teams in Asia, had its golden era during the 1950s and early 1960s.
